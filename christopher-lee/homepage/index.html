About Chris Lee

Most coaches teach skill. Chris Lee teaches what keeps you on the floor.

Chris Lee operates at the intersection of collegiate basketball, professional player development, and applied sport psychology.

As a current college basketball coach, Chris works daily in recruiting, scouting, and game planning inside a conference championship-level program. He also coaches on the Nike EYBL circuit, working with elite high school athletes navigating national exposure and recruiting pressure.

In the professional space, Chris has worked with NBA athletes using film, analytics, and behavioral frameworks to translate knowledge into real decision-making under pressure.

Before coaching, he played professionally in Germany, Brazil, and Peru --experiences that shaped his understanding of adaptation, identity, and long-term success in the game.

Chris is currently completing his Master of Science in Applied Psychology, formalizing the same frameworks he applies every day on the court.

Chris Lee works inside real college programs, real recruiting rooms, and real player development environments --where playing time is earned through decisions, not just talent.
