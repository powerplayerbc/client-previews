The Problem

Why Most Players Stay Stuck

Most players forget what happened in a game within 24 hours. Mistakes repeat because they're never actually processed. Emotions take over, but there's no system to reset or learn from them. Players think they're improving --but they're not tracking anything real. Without reflection, games don't turn into growth.

The Solution

A System That Turns Every Game Into a Lesson

After each game, you'll use the journal to identify where you hesitated, forced decisions, or lost focus. Track the habits that build trust with your coach. Separate highlight plays from winning plays. Recognize patterns in your decision-making over time. Prepare for film sessions with clear, honest self-assessment.
